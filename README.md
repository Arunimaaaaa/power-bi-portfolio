## 🚗 Ola Ride Operations & Performance Dashboard
**`July 2024 · 103,024 Bookings`**

Analyzed one month of Ola's ride data to uncover the root causes behind a **37.9% cancellation rate** — translating operational findings into driver incentive and supply-allocation strategies.

**Highlights:** ₹35M revenue · ₹548 avg fare · 64K completed rides · Banashankari identified as #1 demand zone · Driver-initiated cancellations confirmed as primary failure point

## 👟 Global Sports Footwear Sales & Performance Dashboard
**`2018–2026 · 30,000 Orders`**

Examined 8 years of global sales to find that Lifestyle and Training categories tied for top revenue at $1.84M each — and that the current discount band (13%) has virtually no impact on sales volume.

**Highlights:** $9.08M total revenue · $121 revenue/unit · W-shaped revenue trend reveals 2021 and 2024 market shocks · Online vs. retail channel gap quantified

[**→ View Project**](./02-footwear-analysis/)

## 🌫️ Delhi Air Quality & Pollution Insights
**`Full Year 2025 · 52,560 Hourly Records`**

Built an hourly environmental dashboard showing Delhi's average AQI of 284, with 8K+ hours classified as "Severe." PM2.5 confirmed as the dominant pollutant driver via correlation analysis.

**Highlights:** Max AQI 1,830 · 34K hours in "Moderate" band · November and May are peak danger months · Humidity inversely predicts AQI — enabling early-warning models

[**→ View Project**](./03-delhi-aqi-weather/)
